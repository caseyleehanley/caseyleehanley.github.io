Slow and Steady

December 18, 2022

As it goes with any creative endeavor, the vision at the start can sometimes fail to resemble the finished product. And that's often a good thing! The best products are relentlessly refined over and over again with numerous missteps along the way. I truly believe that's how great things become so great. While I originally imagined a more frequent posting schedule for this blog, it's pretty clear that bar was too high for me (my last post was over 6 months ago). C'est la vie. I venture on anyway!

Over the past 6 months, I've gone back and forth on the title for this blog. The original title was "Objective-C(asey)" with a subtitle of "Adventures in Mac Programming." While I still like the throwback to Objective-C and the old way of programming for Apple platforms, I felt that it was a bit too limiting in scope. I'm interested in a wide variety of things related to technology and computers in general, so restricting it to topics related only to Mac programming felt disingenuous. My intended topics are still similar: nerdy programming- and development-related stuff (and let's be honest, still mostly about Apple), just less specific. The new title is simple and removes any chance of imposing artificial scope limitations. It's just me! This is my little corner of the internet! I did add a subtitle, though. I thought "Connoisseur of Fine Computing" was a fun subtitle that maintained a nice balance of general and specific, highlighting my enthusiasm for computers without pigeon-holing me into a single topic. It's also meant to be a bit silly!

Here's to new ideas and more reasonable expectations. Maybe I'll even write a post related to programming next time! What a thought.
