About Me

Hi, I’m Casey.

I love the Mac and have always aspired to build native software for it. I’m currently working as a senior full-stack engineer at FSB Companies using a mix of native and web technologies. I previously worked at Apple on a variety of web-based tools. While building for the web has been interesting over the years, my heart has always been with the Mac.

Thus far in my career, the extent of my native programming for Apple platforms has been minimal. As I make a more serious effort to become a skilled macOS developer (and iOS/iPadOS/tvOS/watchOS, of course), this blog is intended to chronicle my journey. Hopefully there are people out there with similar backgrounds in web technologies who I can inspire to consider targeting the Mac or other native platorms, taking advantage of all their unique capabilities.

You can follow my app releases over at Native Crane, where I build native apps for Apple platforms.
