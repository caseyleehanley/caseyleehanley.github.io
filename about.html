About Me

Hi, I’m Casey.

I program computers of all shapes and sizes, and have been doing so for over half a decade. I’m currently working at FSB Companies using a mix of native and web technologies to empower small businesses. I previously worked at Apple on a variety of web-based tools.

I'm enthusiastic about the future of the Swift programming language, especially the more widespread adoption of Swift on the server. I'm also very passionate about the Mac, and one day hope to build native software for the platform. My favorite programming languages are Swift and Go, and I've also enjoyed learning Objective-C recently.

You can follow my hobby app releases over at Native Crane, my space for creating fun apps and learning how I can take advantage of all the unique capabilities of Apple platforms.
