Hello World

Hello, world!

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, where I plan to chronicle my adventures in programming. I've never written a blog before, so this will be a little rough at first. Bear with me...

WWDC 2022 is just about to kick off, and I'm excited to see what's been in the works. I'm looking forward to improvements to SwiftUI for the Mac, allowing more developers to share in the Mac renaissance currently being enabled by Apple silicon. It's an exciting time to be a Mac user, and I think it's a perfect time to dive deep into development with Swift and Objective-C.

I'm looking forward to sharing my journey!
